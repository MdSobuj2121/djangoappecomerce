About Us


Neel was born out of a love for wholesome dairy products and a desire to make them accessible to everyone. Our founders, Amith Hassan Anik, envisioned a platform where customers could not only purchase top-notch milk but also connect with the rich stories behind each product. Today, that vision is a reality as we continue to grow and evolve.

Mission


By choosing Neel, you are not just buying milk; you are joining a movement that values quality, diversity, sustainability, and community. Together, let's celebrate the richness of dairy and the joy it brings to our lives.
